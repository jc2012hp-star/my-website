Rajasthan State Certificate in Information Technology

Rajasthan State- Certificate in Information Technology (RS-CIT) course in a strong effort towards spreading digital literacy in the state, upgrading the living standards of citizens through the inclusion of technology, building a digital knowledge-based society and increasing their efficiency.

RS-CIT is a complete digital literacy course designed to help develop basic understanding of computers, learn essential skills and gain confidence in working on computers for home/office work. It provides opportunities to bring more productivity and improve lifestyle through use of technology and prosperity in professional career.

The government of Rajasthan has also considered this course suitable for IT Literacy and it has been recognized by the Department of Information Technology and Communication.
