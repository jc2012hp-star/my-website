RKCL Certified Tally Executive

The Tally Executive course is a job-oriented program designed to equip learners with the essential skills required for entry-level accounting jobs. This course focuses on practical, day-to-day tasks performed by accounting professionals.

You will learn to manage accounts, process transactions, and handle compliance using Tally software. This certification from RKCL validates your skills and makes you a suitable candidate for various finance roles in small and medium-sized businesses.
